# Opening Book Credits

## gm2001.bin

This is the **gm2001** Polyglot opening book, a widely used chess opening book
compiled from Grandmaster-level games. It is distributed freely within the
computer chess community.

**Original compilation:** The gm2001 book was compiled from a collection of
Grandmaster games and converted to the Polyglot `.bin` format for use with
UCI-compatible chess engines.

We gratefully acknowledge the work of the chess community members who compiled
and maintained this opening book resource.

---

Users may load their own Polyglot `.bin` opening books via the GUI
("Load Book..." button in the controls panel).
